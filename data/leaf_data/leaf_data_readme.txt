This folder contains data associated with leaves and sampling sites, including traits, gas exchange, soils and canopy photos.


  CNP/

    extracted and raw CNP data for leaves, soil and litter	

  chlorophyll

    extracted and raw chlorophyll data

  licor/

    processed and raw licor data
 
  gap/

    canopy gap fraction data

  misc/

    LMA, leaf water content, lineage, leaf age and delta 13 C data
