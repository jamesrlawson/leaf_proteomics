environmental_data/

  environmental data associated with sampling sites. These data were not collected in the initial study and         have been sourced from online respositories

leaf_data/

  data associated with leaves and sampling sites, including traits, gas exchange, soils and canopy photos

misc_data/  

  miscellaneous data associated with all samples (species*site ID's, sample lats and longs)

proteomics_data/

  proteomics data associated with samples, including:

    files associated with mercator/MAPMAN protein annotation

    proteomics data: SWATH data, protein assay data, calculated ion areas and protein amounts

    protein sequence files: protein sequence and molecular weight data
